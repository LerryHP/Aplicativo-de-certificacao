About us

We are a Brazilian team of students, brought together by a passion for technology, innovation, and sneaker culture. We created VerifiQ as an academic project to make a secure, accessible way to certify product authenticity. By using blockchain and data analysis, we aim to bring more trust to buyers, sellers, and collectors alike.


This is just the beginning of our journey.
